Transforming Lives, Together

Prometheus School and Sai Kripa’s Journey

Since our inception in 2019, Prometheus School has proudly supported Anjana Ji’s inspiring vision at Sai Kripa. Our recent visit to the orphanage was a testament to this enduring bond and shared commitment to education and community development.

A Testament to Collaboration

During our visit, we witnessed the profound impact of our students’ contributions from the Christmas carnival. These efforts, combined with the generous financial support from our founding chairperson for the school construction project, highlight our unwavering dedication to creating positive change.

Building a Future of Hope and Opportunity

At Prometheus School, we believe in more than just constructing buildings; we are committed to crafting a future filled with hope and opportunity for underprivileged children. Each brick laid and each donation made brings us closer to a brighter tomorrow for the students of Sai Kripa.

A Shared Vision

Our partnership with Sai Kripa is built on a shared vision of transforming lives through education. Anjana Ji’s dedication and passion have inspired us to contribute meaningfully to this cause, ensuring that every child has access to quality education and a supportive learning environment.

The Road Ahead

As we continue this journey together, we remain steadfast in our commitment to fostering education and community development. Our collaboration with Sai Kripa exemplifies the power of unity and shared purpose in making a lasting difference.

Together, Prometheus School and Sai Kripa are not just building a school; we are building a legacy of hope, opportunity, and transformative change for underprivileged children. This journey is a reminder that through collaboration and dedication, we can make a significant impact, one step at a time.
