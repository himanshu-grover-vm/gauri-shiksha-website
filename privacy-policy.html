Privacy Policy

Gauri Shiksha Foundation is founded with an aim to make a brighter, more sustainable future empowered by mutual cooperation and commitment to make conditions livable for one and all. We, as a responsible entity, are committed to respect the privacy of our users who need sharing their personal information with us for various purposes.

Overview of the Privacy Policy

By personal information, we mean:

  • Your name, age, occupation
  • Your contact details – phone number, email address, contact address, etc.
  • Payments related information
  • User ID and password, you choose for login purposes
  • Any other information Gauri Shiksha needs for operational purposes.

By using our website, you agree to terms and conditions related to exchange or furnishing of information. We will collect information for:

  • Enabling donations while site browsing: Navigating or browsing the website will not require you sharing the personal information, but for making donations, etc. you will require furnishing payment details.
  • Generation information pertaining to use: Operating system will automatically collect the browsing session details like time and date of visit, browser used, etc.
  • We treat general information and personal information separately. The general information will be used only for statistical analysis or for experience enhancement purposes.
  • The general information provided may be used by Gauri Shiksha for gauging the user interest and behavioral analysis purposes.

Use of information

Personal information will be used for enabling communications, brochure delivery, annual report, catalogues, and other important information required for seamless operations.

Any purchase of products or donations made will be enabled with the help of personal information.
